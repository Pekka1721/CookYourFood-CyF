Ingredients

  • 2 cups pancake mix
  • 1 teaspoon Ground Cinnamon
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Stir pancake mix and cinnamon in large bowl until well blended. Stir in eggs, milk, oil and vanilla just until blended.
  2. Pour 1/4 cup of batter per pancake onto preheated lightly greased griddle or skillet. Cook 1 to 2 minutes per side or until golden brown, turning when pancakes begin to bubble. Serve pancakes with Spiced Syrup, if desired.
